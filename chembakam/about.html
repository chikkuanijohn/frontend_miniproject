Our Story

Chempakam® is a registered clothing brand, based in Cochin (Kerala – India).

Driven by passion, we have established ourselves as a prominent and preferred online seller of women clothing in the region. With our own production facilities and in-house design team, we create innovative and unique products, at affordable prices.

Customers recognize us for the simplicity & elegance of our materials and unmatched service quality.

We are flexible for everything but quality. The QA team manually checks every piece of work and ensures adherence to the highest standards before it reaches the customers. We do not compromise or negotiate on quality.

Chempakam respects and welcomes our clients’ creativity. You like to have your ideas and dreams transformed into your clothing; you are at the right place. Our designers can help you develop or give finishing touches to your ideas and have it tailored your way.

We are committed to respond to all queries in a prompt and professional manner. Same hour response is guaranteed, if not within the quarter during our working hours.

We ship across India for free and to other countries with a fee.
